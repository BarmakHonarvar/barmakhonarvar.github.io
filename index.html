Academic Profile

Senior Member IEEE & OPTICA | AFHEA | Assistant Professor (Lecturer) with a distinguished track record in digital signal and image processing, machine learning, and medical imaging. Proven leadership as Principal Investigator managing £1.3M+ in research funding from EPSRC, UKRI, GACR, and dstl/ASTRID. Published in top-tier journals (Automatica, IEEE Access, Information Sciences) with 40+ peer-reviewed articles. Extensive teaching experience across undergraduate and postgraduate levels, supervising 1 PhD to completion, 4 current PhD students, and 40+ MSc projects. Committed to securing research income, developing innovative curricula, and advancing AI-driven solutions for healthcare and industrial applications.
